  • Lpi

  • How to improve your LPi score?

    LPi score is sum of various responses given by you along with analysis of your credit report, bank statements etc. But you can increase your score. You have in total 5 chances to make changes and run your LPi.

  • Some of the aspects which boost your scores are:

    1. Addition of co-applicants and their incomes. Subject to our terms & conditions.
    2. Giving correct information about yourself
    3. Uploading updated bank statements
    4. Repaying your loans and credit card bills on time

  • Important Information to be kept in mind:

    1. Your data will not be shared with any of the financers till you click the ‘apply’ button.
    2. Upon clicking of apply button you would not be allowed to change any aspect of your application
