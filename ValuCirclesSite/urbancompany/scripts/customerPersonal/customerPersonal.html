ValuCircles Advice

Any co-owner of the property MUST be a co-applicant on the loan.

In case of sole ownership you may add a co-applicant if the co-applicant:

 - Is an immediate family member

 - Has verifiable income which can be pooled with yours to boost eligibility

 - Is a woman as she may be eligible for special interest rate

In case of sole ownership It is not advisable to add co-applicant if the co-applicant:

 - Is nearing or past retirement age

 - Already has loan obligations to their maximum eligibility
