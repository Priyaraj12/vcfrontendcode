ValuCircles Advice

Loan Amount to Property Value ratio (LTV) is governed by RBI norms. Maximum LTV is 90% for loan amounts up to INR 30 lakhs, 80% for loan amounts of INR 30 lakhs to INR 75 lakhs and 75% for loan amounts greater than INR 75 lakhs.

Eligible tenure of the loan is dependent on age of applicants. With loan tenures ranging from 36 months to 360 months, an applicant's loan tenure will be capped at either 30 years or number of years till the upper cap specified by the lender (upper caps specified by lenders are generally between 60 years to 70 years).
